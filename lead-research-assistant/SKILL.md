# Lead Research Assistant

This skill helps identify and qualify potential leads for your business.

## When to Use This Skill

- Finding potential customers or clients
- Building a list of companies for partnerships
- Identifying target accounts for sales outreach
- Researching companies that match your ideal customer profile
- Preparing for business development activities

## What This Skill Does

1. **Understands Your Business**: Analyzes product/service, value proposition, and target market
2. **Identifies Target Companies**: Finds companies matching your ideal customer profile:
   - Industry and sector
   - Company size and location
   - Technology stack and tools
   - Growth stage and funding
3. **Prioritizes Leads**: Ranks companies based on fit score
4. **Provides Contact Strategies**: Suggests how to approach each lead

## How to Use

### Basic Usage

```
I'm building [product]. Find me 10 companies in [location/industry] that would be good leads.
```

### With Your Codebase

```
Look at what I'm building in this repository and identify companies that would benefit.
```

### Advanced Usage

```
My product: [description]
Ideal customer profile:
- Industry: [industry]
- Company size: [size range]
- Location: [location]
- Must have: [specific requirements]
```

## Research Approach

1. **Web Search**: Find companies matching criteria
2. **Verify Information**: Cross-check company details
3. **Enrich Data**: Gather contact info, recent news
4. **Prioritize**: Rank by fit and readiness

## Output Format

Provide leads with:
- Company name and website
- Industry and size
- Why they're a good fit
- Suggested outreach approach

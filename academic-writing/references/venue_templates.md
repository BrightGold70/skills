# Venue Templates

This document summarizes submission requirements for mainstream academic conferences and journals, used in `switch-venue` mode.

## CS/ML Conferences

| Venue | Page Limit | References | Key Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NeurIPS** | 9 pages | Unlimited | Must include Checklist (appendix) |
| **ICML** | 8 pages | Unlimited | Must include Broader Impact Statement |
| **ICLR** | 9 pages | Unlimited | Must include Ethics Statement, LLM Disclosure |
| **ACL/EMNLP** | 8 pages | Unlimited | Must include Limitations section |
| **AAAI** | 7 pages | 2 pages | Extremely strict formatting,prohibiting modification of style files |

## General Science Journals

| Journal | Type | Length | Key Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nature** | Article | ~3000 words | Emphasizes broad impact, Figure 1 must be extremely polished |
| **Science** | Research Article | ~4500 words | Story-driven narrative, extremely high data visualization quality |
| **IEEE Trans.** | Journal | 10-12 pages | Emphasizes technical details and rigorous mathematical derivation |

## Conversion Recommendations

- **NeurIPS → ICML**: Reduce by 1 page. Consider compressing Related Work or moving detailed proofs to appendix.
- **ICML → ICLR**: Can add 1 page. Consider adding ablation studies or qualitative analysis.
| **General → ACL**: Must add independent Limitations section.
